2018 International Conference on Biomechanics in Movement and Clinical Translation
Call for Abstracts (1st Round)

2018 International Conference on Biomechanics in Movement and Clinical Translation（ICBMC' 17）will be held at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai ,China from June 22 to 24, 2018.

The conference program will include lectures given by international renowned experts and presentations of other researchers from China and overseas, a workshop that demonstrates the methods of using motion capture system and relative software will be followed. The ICBMC Executive Committee is now accepting abstracts for podium presentation and scientific posters. High quality research papers (presentations) will be honored as the excellent papers to encourage the students and young researchers in biomechanics in human movement and clinical application.

(I) Topic List: (including but not limited to the following topics)
Musculoskeletal Biomechanics
Gait and posture
Clinical biomechanics
Injury and Rehabilitation Biomechanics
Foot and Ankle Biomechanics
Prosthetics & Robotics
Sports Biomechanics
Modeling/Simulation
(II) Abstracts Submission Rules & Guidelines

Abstracts Submission Methods:

In order to further standardize the submission, review & publication proceedings of conference papers, the conference will submit a summary of the conference papers by below website access. Please visit the conference website access at link(https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=sicbmc2017) & submit your paper according to the related process.( Please create your system account by click above link first & log in your account , click above link again &then you can upload your abstracts by related detailed steps.)

If specific individual authors have difficulties or cannot successfully submit your papers through the website, please submit your abstract by sending it to our conference submission public mailbox(dmctgait@126.com)

All conference abstracts will be reviewed by our conference academic committee of the General Assembly, and then will be decided its presentation form (podium or poster). Authors will receive notice of paper acceptance by our conference by DATE.

The schedule of podium and poster presentation with their requirements will be enclosed in the notification of paper acceptance letter.

Format requirements for conference paper abstracts:

The detailed summary of the paper in either Chinese or English version will be accepted by this conference. Manuscripts will be original research related. Please log on the online submission system for submission of your paper.

(III) High-Quality Research Paper Awards

This award aims to recognize significant contributions from new investigators in human movement analysis and allied professions. Some international & domestic renowned experts in related fields will be members of conference paper review & award committee. The high-quality paper presentations & paper posters will be awarded.
